We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for regular ceremonies. For peak seasons like Paryushan Parva, Navpad Oli, and major festivals, booking 1-2 months ahead is advisable to ensure availability.

Yes! Shrey is available for events across India. Travel arrangements and accommodation for out-of-city events can be discussed during the booking process. Many temples and families invite Shrey from different states for their ceremonies.

Shrey specializes in all major Jain puja ceremonies including Snatra Puja, Panch Kalyanak, Sattar Bhed, Shashti Vidhi, Navpad Oli, Paryushan events, Mangal Divo, Jinabhishek, and many more. Custom ceremonies can also be arranged based on your requirements.

Duration varies by puja type. A Mangal Divo or Aarti is typically 30-45 minutes, while comprehensive pujas like Snatra Puja take 2-3 hours, and grand ceremonies like Panch Kalyanak can be 4-5 hours. Exact timings are discussed during booking.

Absolutely! Shrey performs at temples, community halls, private residences, and any appropriate venue. Whether it's an intimate family puja or a large community event, every ceremony receives the same level of devotion and professionalism.

Shrey comes with traditional instruments for the ceremony. For larger events, sound equipment arrangements can be discussed separately. Many venues already have their own sound systems which Shrey can work with seamlessly.
